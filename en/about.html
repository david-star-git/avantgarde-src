Our Story

Hello, I'm Sebastian Starinski, the founder of Avantgarde. After many years of practice and countless further training courses, I thought to myself in 2018: "Why not?" and became self-employed. This is how Avantgarde was founded in Austria, focusing on property development and building services.

Two years later, I was drawn to Paraguay – and don't worry, the company came with me.

Since 2021, we have been building primarily German-style single-family houses here. Our success? We owe it to our wonderful customers who trusted us and enabled our growth. We now have three construction teams ready to tackle any building adventure.

After ten single-family houses, numerous garages, pools, terraces, and roofs, we look optimistically to the future. I get immense pleasure from accompanying families in planning their new home and seeing their dreams come true.

We look forward to welcoming you soon for a consultation – and perhaps spreading a little construction magic.

"Building a home is more than construction – it's about creating a space where families grow, memories are made, and dreams come true."
— Sebastian Starinski, Founder

Our Core Values

Excellent Service

For every project, we create a professional plan with 3D views. We stay in constant contact throughout the entire construction phase and keep you informed about the most important construction progress.

Security

Before starting a project, we conclude a construction contract with a notary, and you pay according to construction progress. Your investment is protected every step of the way.

Quality

We build solid brick houses with stable foundation slabs, thick exterior walls, and wooden roof structures, using high-quality materials with the best workmanship.

Transparency

Clear communication, honest pricing, and regular updates ensure you're always informed and confident in your investment.

Sustainability

We use durable materials and efficient building techniques that stand the test of time while respecting the environment.

Innovation

Combining traditional German building standards with modern design and technology for homes that are both timeless and contemporary.
