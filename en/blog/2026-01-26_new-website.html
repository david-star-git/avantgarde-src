Welcome to Our New Website!

2026-01-26 1 min read
Announcement
website launch update

We’re excited to welcome you to our new website.

Over the past weeks, we’ve been working behind the scenes on a complete refresh. What started as a simple, single-page site has grown into a more structured and flexible website that better reflects who we are and how we work. The goal was clear: make the site easier to navigate, faster to load, and more pleasant to use.

What’s new

Faster and smoother experience
Pages now load more quickly, images appear sooner, and the overall experience feels lighter and more responsive - especially on slower connections or mobile devices.

Cleaner, more consistent design
We’ve simplified the layout and improved consistency across the site. This makes content easier to read, helps important information stand out, and creates a more professional overall look.

Better structure and navigation
The website now consists of multiple pages, allowing us to organize content more clearly. Finding information is easier, and moving between sections feels more natural.

Improved language support
Each language now has its own dedicated pages, making translations clearer, safer, and more reliable - without relying on workarounds that could cause display issues.

A new blog section
We now support blog posts directly on our website. This allows us to share updates, news, and insights more regularly, all in one place.

Still improving

While this launch is a big step forward, the website is very much a living project. We’ll continue making improvements, refining details, and adding new content over time to ensure it serves our visitors as well as possible.

Your feedback matters

If you spot anything that doesn’t work as expected or have ideas on how we can improve the site, we’d love to hear from you. Your feedback helps us keep moving in the right direction.

Thank you for visiting - and welcome to our new website.
